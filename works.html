Projects

Below are a few projects I am most proud of. Two of them are coded websites, and two of them are full mock-ups made in Sketch. All of my coded pieces can be found on my GitHub.

The Hub

The Hub is an Angular 4 application that uses GitHub and Firebase APIs to allow a user to login/create an account and search for users/repositories as well as create and join chat rooms with other GitHub users. It was our group week project for the JavaScript track at Epicodus. My partner and I wanted the user to feel relaxed and have ease navigating through the site. We got inspired by the desktop site of GitHub. We wanted to roll with that without taking GitHub's original branding and without the use of Octocat. I created the planet and the stars on the landing page with pure CSS. We had 5 days to complete it, with five different pages, it was the biggest project I've worked on so far.
